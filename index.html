Who Are We

Bombay Hampers was inspired by a legacy that began in 1981 with a family store filled with fragrant spices and warm customer connections. Growing up in that environment, founder Kevin C. Furia learned the value of thoughtful details and genuine relationships. Carrying this forward, he envisioned a way to turn simple gestures into memorable experiences. In 2022, that vision became Bombay Hampers - a brand rooted in Mumbai’s warmth, resilience, and endless creativity. Each hamper is more than a collection of products, it’s a story carefully curated to reflect your occasion, values, and personality. From corporate branding and festive celebrations to intimate milestones, we design and deliver hampers that delight, connect, and leave a lasting impression. Our differentiators lie in custom branding, premium packaging design, and end-to-end service—from concept and curation to nationwide and international delivery. With every box, we combine trusted quality, local craftsmanship, and seamless service—helping you celebrate life’s moments with care and meaning.

Why Bombay Hampers?

At Bombay Hampers, our difference lies in the way we truly listen. We treat every client with the highest respect,valuing their ideas, and vision as the foundation of what we create together.
We don’t just make hampers; we help you bring your story to life through thoughtful concepts, innovative product choices, and elegant curations. By collaborating closely, we ensure each hamper is a reflection of your unique style and purpose—setting your gifts apart with creativity and class that truly resonates.

Clientele & Partnerships

Trusted by icons. Chosen by brands.

With gratitude and humility, Bombay Hampers has grown from a simple idea into a cherished experience of thoughtful giving. We’ve had the joy of adding a little extra care to moments for Bollywood favourites like Kareena Kapoor, Ayushmann Khurrana, Jeetendra Kapoor and Shilpa Shetty.

Over the years, we’ve partnered with 1500+ families, brands & corporates—including Kalki Fashion, Alpino, HDFC ERGO, Party Smart India, Foundever, Global Indulge, Quintessentially, East Vantage and NMIMS Law School—building relationships on respect, trust and shared vision. We also welcome foreign delegates from the UK, Canada, USA, Japan, Malaysia and the Philippines, crafting hampers that celebrate India’s warmth and culture.

We’re honoured to be a gifting partner at the world’s grandest Garba festival with Falguni Pathak. Through every collaboration, our promise remains the same: gifts that touch hearts, build connections and leave lasting memories.
